Delivery Fees

Delivery fees are calculated based on the distance between the restaurant and your delivery address. Orders over ₹500 qualify for free delivery within a 5km radius.

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your order for a full refund up until the restaurant accepts and begins preparing it. Once preparation has started, a 100% cancellation fee will apply.

Hygiene & Safety

All our delivery partners follow strict hygiene protocols, including regular temperature checks and mandatory mask-wearing. You can opt for "Contactless Delivery" at checkout.
